Pokémon

Combat Game

Rules:

    1. Choose your 3 Pokémon.
    2. Once you have your assembled your team, you will battle the AI's randomly selected Pokémon.
    3. The winner is determined based on the Pokémon's speed, attack, defense, and base experience stats.
    4. After the battle is over, we'll recommend something else for you to do... or you can play again!
